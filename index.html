About us

We would like to familiarize you a little with our Specialty. Our snacks are fresh and according to taste of both British bakery and Indian spicy snacks. We use fresh creams and veggies. All dishes are spiced and digestible by our chef. If you like to eat spicy or medium spicy food, do not hesitate to state this when placing your order.

We also offer a variety of vegetarian and English creams. Ask us and we will be happy to give you more information about the ingredients used in our dishes. Since we prepare all dishes fresh for you, there may be a short wait. We wish you a pleasant stay and above all a good appetite!
